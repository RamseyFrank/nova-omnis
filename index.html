Exploring Housing Market Trends Through Data

Project Overview

This project analyzes housing affordability and market trends in the Austin, Texas metro area from 2018 to 2024. It integrates Excel for initial data cleaning, SQL for structured querying, and Power BI for dashboard visualization. The goal is to provide insights into housing prices, rent levels, mortgage burdens, and income growth to better understand affordability shifts in a rapidly evolving market.
